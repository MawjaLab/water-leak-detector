# Water Leak Detector – YouTube Series

## Series Goal

The viewer should be able to follow the complete development process from the initial idea to the validated result.

## Content Guidelines

- Justify decisions technically
- Document mistakes transparently
- Distinguish assumptions from measurements
- Cite datasheet references
- Never fabricate measurement results
- Show the development process, not just the final result
